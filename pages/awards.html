PACKAGE PANIC

Awards

Below is a list of all awards that can be earned while playing Package Panic. Some are automatic, others are manually awarded by the developer. Good luck collecting them all!

CODE OF ARMS

Awarded for reporting a real glitch in the game.

ROYAL ARTIST

Awarded to design contest winners.

LAB RAT

Awarded to all beta play testers.

PANIC SURVIVOR

Awarded for winning a level at 95% Panic or higher.

THINKING OUTSIDE THE BOX

Awarded quarterly to the players who submit the best ideas for improving the game.

GOLD/SILVER/BRONZE CUP

Awarded monthly to players with the TOP 3 spots on the leaderboard for each level.

Speedy Delivery

Awarded quarterly to the best social media posts advertising the game.

PLATINUM CUP

Awarded weekly to the top overall leaderboard leader.

FRANK AWARD

Awarded for surviving >1 minute in Area51.

BRONZE/SILVER/GOLD PACKAGE

Awarded at (1000), (2500), and (5000) correct package placements across any number of levels.

JUGGERNAUT STAR

Awarded for beating all 50 levels.

BINGE WATCHER

Awarded for watching 50 ads.

SHINY/USED/ANTIQUE CONVEYOR

Awarded at 10, 25, and 50 hours of play.
